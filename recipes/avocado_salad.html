Avocado Salad

Description

Avocado salad is a delicious and healthy dish made with ripe avocados and a variety of fresh vegetables and herbs. It is a popular choice for those who are looking for a healthy and flavorful meal, as it is high in vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats. It can be served as a side dish or as a main course, depending on the portion size and accompanying ingredients. Here is a simple recipe you can try out for you and your family.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium English cucumber, cut into quarters through the length and then sliced
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 small red onion, sliced into small pieces
  • 2 medium avocados (firm but ripe), sliced into bite size pieces

    • For the dressing

  • 1 1/2 tbsp fresh lemon juice (can also use lime juice)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 3 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. For the dressing: In a small mixing bowl whisk together lemon juice, red wine vinegar, extra virgin oil oil, honey, garlic, cilantro, parsley, oregano, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. In a large bowl gently toss together cucumbers, tomatoes, red onion, and avocado with dressing.
  3. Serve shortly after preparing.
