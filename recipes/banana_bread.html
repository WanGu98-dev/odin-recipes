Banana bread

Description

Banana bread is a popular dessert that combines the natural sweetness of ripe bananas with the soft and fluffy texture of cake. It's a great way to use up ripe bananas that might otherwise go to waste and makes a delicious treat for any occasion. This is my favorite recipe as it is easy to make and ingredients are almost always available. However, if you like, you could add extra ingredients such as nuts, dried fruit and chocolate chunks. There are so many ways to make banana cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 overripe bananas (peeled)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup castor sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F) and butter the loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until completely smooth.
  3. Add your oil, milk, eggs, brown sugar, castor sugar and vanilla essence and mix completely
  4. Sieve in your flower and baking powder
  5. Add salt and mix completely
  6. Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan and put it in the oven.
  7. Bake for 50 - 60 minutes at 160°C (320 F).
  8. Cool and serve!
